# Privacy Policy for Syinx

**Last updated: July 2, 2026**

## Summary

Syinx collects no personal data. Nothing leaves your browser.

## Data We Collect

**None.**

Syinx stores the following data **locally on your device only**, in `chrome.storage.local`:

- Your prompt history (last 50 prompts you typed)
- Your settings (which sites are selected, auto-submit preference)

This data is:

- Never transmitted to any server
- Never shared with any third party
- Never used for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose
- Completely under your control — you can clear it at any time from the extension

## Permissions We Request

| Permission                                                     | Why                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| -------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `storage`                                                      | To save your prompt history and settings locally on your device                                                                                                                           |
| `tabs`                                                         | To detect whether a ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini tab is already open, and to switch to or open one                                                                                               |
| `host_permissions` (chatgpt.com, claude.ai, gemini.google.com) | To insert your typed prompt into those sites' input boxes on your behalf. We only **write** the prompt text you gave us — we never read, store, or transmit any content from those pages. |

## Network Activity

Syinx makes **zero network requests**. It has no backend server. It does not phone home. It does not contain analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting.

The only "network activity" is your browser's normal navigation to chatgpt.com, claude.ai, or gemini.google.com — which you control.
